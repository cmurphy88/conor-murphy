## The Brief

Ann runs a bakery in Fermanagh specialising in wedding cakes and celebration cakes, and also runs baking workshops for hen parties, baby showers, and corporate team days. She had no website and was relying entirely on Instagram and word of mouth.

She needed two things from the site: a professional portfolio that brides could share with their partners, and a bookings page that made it easy for people to secure a spot on a workshop without needing to go back and forth on messages.

## What We Built

The site is built as two connected experiences under one roof.

**The wedding cake portfolio** is a clean, image-led showcase of Ann's work — cakes by style, flavour, and occasion. At the heart of it is a custom enquiry form that lets brides upload inspiration images alongside their message. That one feature alone saves Ann hours of back-and-forth, because enquiries arrive with everything she needs to give an accurate quote.

**The workshops page** lists upcoming dates with full details — what's included, group sizes, how to book — and lets visitors secure their place directly. No phone calls, no DMs, no "I'll get back to you."

## Key Features

- Custom enquiry form with inspiration image uploads
- Workshop booking page with date listings
- Mobile-first design built around how brides and workshop guests actually browse
- Fast load times — images are optimised so the gallery never drags
- Professional email setup: `hello@bakedbyann.co.uk`

## The Result

Within weeks of launching, Ann was receiving regular enquiries through the site from brides who had found her on Google — not just Instagram. The workshops page has been particularly effective for hen party bookings, with groups able to browse available dates and book without needing to message first.

> "I had no website at all before this. Now brides contact me through the site every week and the workshops page has been brilliant for hen parties. It's paid for itself many times over."
> — Ann, Owner
